We came upon the gallows.

It stood empty.

That detail should not matter. And yet, in Barovia, absence rarely means nothing. The rope stirred slightly despite the still air. I observed no wind, no movement, no visible cause. Only motion without origin.

Then the dead appeared.

Not corpses—impressions. Figures drawn from the earth itself, shaped like those who had walked this land before us. Armed. Silent. Purposeful. Each one moved in the same direction.

Toward the castle.

No hesitation. No divergence. A procession defined not by life, but by conclusion. Failed attempts given form and set again upon the same path.

The Vistana spoke as we watched.

Not with surprise. With certainty.

“What may be. Not what will.”

Accurate. Divination does not reveal outcomes. Only possibilities constrained by pattern.

“I can tell you what you must do, but I cannot ensure that you will succeed.”

Also accurate.

Then she pointed.

Not broadly. Not symbolically.

At me.

I acknowledged it without comment. The implication required none.

---

### The Reading

The cards were drawn.

I observed structure, not mysticism.

Each position aligned with function.

**First — History**

An heir. A noble lineage. An ancient enemy.

Redundant with known variables. Strahd remains the central axis. However, the mention of lost souls as guides introduces an additional layer—either literal interaction with the dead, or a recursive pattern in which failure informs future attempts.

Neither interpretation suggests advantage.

---

**Second — Protection**

A holy symbol.

Location defined by contradiction: wolves, hills, a lake, and association with Mother Night.

Protective artifacts do not naturally reside within predatory domains.

Conclusion:

- It was placed there
- It was taken there
- Or it is held by something that should not possess it

Retrieval will require force or disruption. Not discovery.

---

**Third — The Marionette**

“Find the beating heart of the castle.”

This is not metaphor.

I have seen this before. Not here, but prior to entering the Mists.

A chamber. Elevated. Containing a heart sustained beyond natural limits.

Previously categorized as abstraction.

Reclassification required.

---

**Final Statement**

“He waits nearby. This is where you will find the Devil.”

Conclusion: proximity.

Strahd is not distant. He is within operational reach already.

---

### The Favor

A request followed.

A child—Arabelle. A birthday in seven days. A toy.

Payment was offered in advance.

This was not incidental.

Favors establish obligation. Obligation creates structure. Even trivial exchanges generate alignment.

We accepted.

---

### The Crossing

We were directed between two trees.

I examined the boundary.

Arcane saturation was present—dense, unstable, unanchored. No identifiable destination. No fixed point of reference.

Conclusion: uncontrolled displacement through mist-mediated transition.

I advised caution.

The group proceeded.

Brennick entered first. Immediate disappearance.

Van followed. Then Orianna.

At that stage, refusal would result in isolation.

Isolation is not survivable here.

I entered.

---

### Transit

No sensation.

No movement.

Only discontinuity.

Reality resumed in a different configuration.

Consistent with mist-based spatial manipulation. Mechanism remains undefined.

---

### After

The encampment was gone.

The trees were inert.

The environment had reset without trace of origin.

Discussion followed. I disclosed the prior vision of the castle’s heart. Reactions varied. All rational. None actionable.

---

### Conclusion

We proceed toward Vallaki.

Identified threads:

- A relic within territory claimed by wolves
    
- A structure within Ravenloft containing a literal heart
    
- A child tied to the Vistani through obligation
    

These are not separate problems.

They will converge.

---

### Final Observation

The dead walked without resistance.

No hesitation. No doubt. No deviation from their path.

That, more than anything, is what I intend to avoid.
