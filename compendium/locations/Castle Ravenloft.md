An immense fortress of black stone, perched atop the sheer cliffs of the Pillarstone, very close to [[Village of Barovia|Village Barovia]]. Can be seen from the village, as a dark background, gray skies and [[The Mists and the Domains of Dread|Mists]] surrounding it - oppressive, grand and watchful.

**Style:** Brutalist gothic architecture—tall spires, pointed arches, narrow windows.  
**Material:** Black granite quarried from the mountains; obsidian inlays; iron-bound gates.  
**Scale:** Monolithic. The highest tower spears the sky like a black blade.

From [[Alaric Voss|Alaric]]'s vision, seems to be where [[Count Strahd von Zarovich|Count Strahd]] resides.
