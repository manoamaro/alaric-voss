Servant of [[Count Strahd von Zarovich|Count Strahd]], he was first seen on the encounter with the Party at [[Session 10]], driving the carriage of his Lord.

# Known Facts
- Age unknown
- Probably a [[Vampiric Entities|vampire]], turned by [[Count Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] himself
- He possesses no detectable enchantment, yet evokes a subtle enthrallment.
- His beauty defies category—symmetry refined beyond mortal proportion. Those in his presence react with quiet awe, as if witnessing an aesthetic truth too pure for comfort.
