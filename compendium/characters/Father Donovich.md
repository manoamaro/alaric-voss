Donavich is the local priest to the Village of Barovia . He resides in the Church in Village of Barovia with his son Doru , now a Vampire spawn.

A little more than a year ago, twenty-year-old Doru and several other villagers stroned Castle Ravenloft in revolt, having benn lured there by a wizard in black robes who came to Barovia from a faraway land. By all accounts, the wizard died by Strahd’s hand, and so too did Doru, who returned to his father as a Vampire spawn . Donavich was able to trap his son in a church’s undercroft, where he remains to this day.

Father Donovich has been feeding his son his own blood since he was imprisoned, but he still cried out to his father at all hours for more. Donavich prays day and night, hoping the gods will tell him how to save Doru without destroying him.

Father Donovich is a priest of Morninglord religion.

After the Party found Doru they killed him in the Undercroft. That night Father Donovich took his own life by hanging himself from the bell tower only for Count Strahd Von Zarovich to force his spirit back into his body. Strahd told Donovich that twice he had disobeyed his will and if he did again he would have Strahd's attention.
